About Us

The Nexly Network provides Minecraft players a place to enjoy nostalgic gamemodes. Our flagship game, Super Smash Blocks, is a remake of the popular Super Craft Brothers gamemode. However, we also provide gamemodes like Parkour and McTheWalls. We hope to see you online!
