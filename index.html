About Us

The Nexly Network provides Minecraft players a place to enjoy nostalgic gamemodes. Our flagship game, Super Smash Blocks, is a remake of the popular Super Craft Brothers gamemode. Nexly Network is proud to have staff that are well-versed with the knowledge and workings of this gamemode, with the result being a polished game that you are sure to enjoy. We hope to see you online!
